Obada's Story

I'm Obada, a versatile videographer and photographer. My journey started with capturing captivating landscapes and evolved into a mission to spotlight the stories of Syrian refugees in Jordan. This experience transformed my perspective, driving me to create impactful visuals. Today, I specialize in commercial videography, documentaries, YouTube content, and music videography. My lens is my voice, and I use it to connect with audiences and send powerful messages. Join me as I continue this visual journey, one frame at a time.
